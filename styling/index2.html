About Me

I am an African. My career started as a graphic designer then on to becoming a UI/UX Designer with a good grasp of frontend development.
I have a passion for bringing lasting change to the many problems in Africa and I believe Tech is one way by which that can be achieved. In a bid to see this change I have contributed to drive this change in Agrotech startup companies and Edutech startup companies.
Organization, attention to details and team collaboration are my strong points.
I love to read (especially biographies and history books). I also love taking long walk in the cool of the day either meditating or speaking with a loved one.
